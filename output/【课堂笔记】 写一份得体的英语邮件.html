【课堂笔记】 写一份得体的英语邮件



1. Begin with a greeting

"Dear _______________"

"Good morning / afternoon / evening _______________"

"Hi_____________"

"Dear Sir / Madam"

"To whom it may concern"


2. Thank the recipient

"Thank you for contacting us about..."

"Thank you for sending your previous email."

"Thank you for your prompt reply to my previous email."

"I appreciate you getting back to me..."

"Thanks for your previous email."


3. State your purpose


4. Add closing remarks

"Thank you again for your time and cooperation."

"Thank you for your work thus far on this project."

"Thanks again and if you have any questions, please feel free to contact me anytime."

"If you have any questions or concerns, don't hesitate to let me know."

"I look forward to hearing from you soon."


5. End with a closing

"Sincerely, (name)"

"Regards (name)"

"Best regards, (name)"

"Have a great day, (name)"

"Respectfully, (name)"
