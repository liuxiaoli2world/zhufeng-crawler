【课堂笔记】学英语时要小心的五个常见错误

课程总结如下，英文部分来自视频原话。相关的举例和详细解说，可以看视频。


Mistake No.1

Trying to do too much in a short period of time. 

试图短时间完成太多事。

When you do this you eventually feel burned out. In other words, your desire to study English slowly goes away.Cramming this will cause you to feel burned out or tired and exhausted quickly when you study English.

急于求成只会让你事倍功半。换句话说，这会让你慢慢失去学习的欲望。填鸭式的死记硬背只会让你在学英语上感到疲惫。

Sometimes as a student you try to cram or put everything in at one time but instead of doing that you should break out your English study into chunks.

作为一个学生，经常会试图在短时间临时抱佛脚，但请不要这样做，要分段式学习。

So we have vocabulary, grammar, listening, writing and speaking. So there are different types of chunks. How to break it down based on time or based on days. You can choose the specific chunk for each day.

英语学习分为不同的板块，有词汇、语法、听力、写作和口语。你可以根据自己的习惯按时间分段，每天学习练习的一个板块。


Mistake No.2

Trying to look up every single vocabulary word you see in. 

试图去查每个生词的意思。

You can avoid making this mistake by using the focus method. Using this method, your first goal is to listen for or redefined the main focus topic and then the supporting information or details about the topic.

可以采用专注法来避免这个问题。首先，你要专注于听素材的中心思想，找到支持主题的细节。

You're going to find the main idea, in other words, the focus or subject then you're going to look for supporting points you can listen for the 5Ws. I'm not saying never to look up vocabulary words but I'm saying first find the important information.

你要找到中心思想，换句话说，就是主旨或主题。可以用5W方法来找支持信息。首要任务是找到重要信息，其次才是查生词。


Mistake No.3

Trying to see how good or bad you are in English based on another English learner's capability or ability.

试图用别人的能力来判断自己的英语水平。

When you do this you actually can discourage yourself from trying harder to learn English.

这样做会使你阻碍自己的学习的积极性。

You can avoid making this mistake by placing more focus on your own English learning journey on a daily weekly or monthly basis. You can write down where you improved and where you need to improve. Don't compare yourself just check your own progress.

你可以通过把注意力放在自己的学习进度上来避免这个问题。每天、每周或者每个月记录的学习成果和有待提高的地方。只是检查进度，不要去和别人做比较。


Mistake No.4

Not making a schedule. This involves basically studying English with no plans or goals. 

缺乏学习计划，包括在没有计划和目标的情况下学习。

Plans and goals are so important when you do this. It actually becomes harder for you to stay motivated to continue studying English.

没有计划和目标的学习，会让你很快失去动力。

Creating a simple study schedule.Your schedule can be based on a daily weekly or monthly plan but focus on making a weekly schedule first so that you can get in the habit of studying.

制定一个简单的学习日程，可以是以每天、每周或每月为单位。可以首先制定一个周计划，有助于你养成学习的习惯。


Mistake No.5

Studying only one aspect of English for a long period of time. 

长期只练习英语的一部分。

For example, only studying grammar and neglecting listening, speaking, writing and vocabulary. By studying only one for a long period of time you inevitably or unavoidably become weaker in all of the other aspects. This will cause you to neglect the other aspects. 

比如，只练习语法而忽视听力、口语、写作和词汇。只专注一部分比会让你顾此失彼。

Remember to balance your study time. You can avoid making the mistake of studying only one aspect by allotting a specific amount of time or setting aside time for each aspect.

记住去合理分配你的学习时间。通过为英语学习的每一部分留出特定的练习时间，避免出现“偏科”的问题。
