【课堂笔记】英语阅读练习的七个好方法

课程总结如下，英文部分来自视频原话。相关的举例和详细解说，可以看视频。


Method No.1

Read out loud.

大声朗读

Read the text passage book or a blog in a good speaking voice. Try to be loud but don't scream, just loud enough to hear yourself. 

用大到自己可以听见的声音朗读文章。

There are three benefits to this method, the first benefit is you become more engaged with the information because you are using your eyes and your ears. The second benefit is it helps you improve your pronunciation because you will begin to listen to yourself more intently. The more you read out loud, the more you will listen to yourself and you'll begin to see which words you're pronouncing correctly and which words you're not pronouncing correctly.The third benefit is reading out loud actually helps to improve your comprehension. It helps you to internalize the words you are reading.

这样做会有三个好处。首先，眼睛耳朵并用，会使你更专注于信息。其次，听见自己的声音，额可以帮助你提高发音。你会听到自己哪些单词发音准，哪些不准。最后，有助于提高你的阅读理解能力，因为你正在消化读到的每个单词。


Method No.2

Read various topics.

读各种话题

After recognizing what topics you are interested in look for English material about those topics but remember to not simply focus on one topic. You want to get a variety of different topics

找到与你感兴趣的话题有关的英语材料，但不要只读这个话题，还要去读其他话题的材料。

You will be exposed to a wide variety of vocabulary words so your vocabulary will also improve.  Number two, you will not become bored with your English studies because you will have different topics to read about. Finally, you will begin to make connections between the various topics.

这样做首先会使你接触各种各样的词汇，有助于提高你的词汇量。其次，读不同的题材会让你觉得英语学习不无聊。最后，你会将不同的题材联系起来。


Method No.3

Read new words.

读新单词

When you see a vocabulary word that you have never seen before repeat the word five to ten times out loud. If you don't know the pronunciation, you can find it in the dictionary and then repeat after listening to the audio file.

当读到新单词时，大声重复5～10次，重复练习发音。

You will begin to recognize which part of the word is difficult to pronounce. What happens is when you first save the word, you're going to realize and notice that your tongue or your mouth has a hard time pronouncing certain parts of that word. The next time you say that you will actually focus on that part so that you can say it better. You will gain confidence in your ability to say the word, which will increase your fluency.

这样做会让你注意到单词中发音困的部分。你会发现自己的舌头和嘴巴对某个单词有发音困难，你就会多加练习并在下一次说得更好。这会提升你的自信心以及流利程度。


Method No.4

Read often daily.

每天练习

Try to read as often as possible you can choose to read every day or every other day but it is important to set aside a specific amount of time for reading.

尽可能多多阅读，每天或每隔一天，最重要的是一定要留出特定的阅读时间。

You can read for five minutes and then move up to twenty or thirty minutes. It all depends on your schedule.

可以先从5分钟开始，然后增加到20～30分钟，这都取决于你的日程。


Method No.5

Read with a friend.

组队阅读

Find a friend who is also an English Learner and pick an English book or blog to read together with them. You can take turns reading a sentence a paragraph or a page each.

找一个也在练习英语的朋友，选一篇文章一起读。可以是轮流读一句话、或是一页。

This will help you with your listening your speaking and your comprehension. It is going to be more enjoyable because you're not doing it alone. You will recognize how your friend reads and you will help them improve, which will also help you improve. 

这样做有助于提高听力、口语和理解水平。组队联系也会让学习更有趣，注意朋友是如何阅读的也能对你有所提高。


Method No.6

Read three times.

读三遍

This method requires you to read the same thing more than once, however this focuses more on reading an entire passage or page more than once.

你需要多次阅读一篇文章。

This will greatly improve your comprehension skills. It will help you with proper pronunciation and fluency and help you recognize and then become familiar with new vocabulary words patterns and sentence structures.

这会大大提高你的阅读理解能力，帮助你练习正确的发音并提高流畅度，还能让你对单词句式更加熟悉。


Method No.7

Read your level.

读适合你的文章

You need to find English reading material that you can understand. After skimming or reading over briefly over the information see if you can understand at least 50%.

你要找你能理解的阅读素材，合适的素材应该是在你泛读后至少能理解一半内容的文章。

So you will not feel as stressed and you will improve your reading ability at the proper pace. Sometimes when you're studying English and you're trying to improve your reading skill, you want to jump too fast but you need to do it at a natural pace. Focus on reading at your level and then you will improve steadily. 

这样做，你就不会感到压力，英语阅读能力会稳步上升。当你在学习英语，想要提高阅读能力时，你想急于求成，但你得按照一个节奏来进行，专注于读适合的英语程度的文章，然后你才能扎实地进步。
